The Line

The Line is a futuristic city project in NEOM, Saudi Arabia. It is designed to be a 170 km-long, 200m-wide, and 500m-tall linear city with no cars, no streets, and zero carbon emissions. The city aims to accommodate 9 million residents, powered by 100% renewable energy, and prioritize nature, AI-driven services, and walkability.

Quick facts about the Line:

  1. Budget: The project is part of the larger NEOM initiative, which has an estimated budget of $500 billion.
  2. Duration: Initial plans aimed for The Line to be fully operational by 2030. However, current projections indicate that only a 1.5-mile section, known as the "Hidden Marina," will be completed by that time.
  3. Workforce: As of May 2024, approximately 140,000 workers are actively engaged in various NEOM projects, including The Line. This number is expected to increase to 200,000 by 2025.
